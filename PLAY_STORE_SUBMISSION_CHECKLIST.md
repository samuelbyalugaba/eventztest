# Google Play Submission Checklist

## Build

- Build a signed Android App Bundle: `android/app/build/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab`.
- Confirm package name: `com.eventz.app`.
- Confirm target SDK: `36`.
- Confirm production version code/name.
- Uninstall older test builds before device testing to avoid cached icons/assets.

## Store Listing

- App name: Eventz.
- Short description: Discover events, follow creators, buy tickets, and join live experiences.
- Full description: include event discovery, creator profiles, tickets, community feed, messaging, and live events.
- Upload phone screenshots for Events, Event Detail, Feed, Profile, Create Event, and Tickets.
- Add app icon, feature graphic, privacy policy URL, support email, and website.

## App Content

- Complete Data Safety.
- Complete Content Rating.
- Complete Target Audience.
- Complete App Access with reviewer credentials or demo path.
- Complete Ads declaration.
- Complete Financial Features / Payments declarations if shown.

## Policies To Verify

- Account deletion is available in app and publicly at `/delete-account`.
- Terms and Privacy Policy are accessible before sign-in and in Settings.
- UGC has report and block options.
- Paid virtual access is disabled on Android unless Play Billing or an approved exception is configured.
- Physical event ticketing copy does not imply Google-managed fulfillment.

## Reviewer Demo Path

1. Open app.
2. Sign in with Google or demo credentials.
3. Browse Events.
4. Open an event detail page.
5. Open Feed and report/block from a non-owned post.
6. Open Profile, Settings, Privacy & Security, and Account Deletion.
7. Create a free event or inspect creator setup if reviewer account is allowed.
